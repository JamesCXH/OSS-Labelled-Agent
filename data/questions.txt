## Important Notes
- Specialty pizzas have default toppings and are not found in the regular pizza builder
- Gluten Free crust is only available in small size
- Handmade Pan crust is only available in medium size
- Crunchy Thin Crust is only available in medium and large sizes
- New York Style crust is only available in medium, large, and x-large sizes
- Water is only available in 20oz size
